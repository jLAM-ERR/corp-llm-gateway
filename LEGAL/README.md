## Licensing model (open core)

- **Core (this repository):** Apache License 2.0 — free for all use,
  including commercial. See root `LICENSE` and `NOTICE`.
- **Enterprise plugins + support:** proprietary, sold separately
  under per-customer written agreements. See
  `COMMERCIAL-LICENSING.md`.
- **Inbound contributions:** exclusive-license CLA (`CLA.md`) — this
  is what preserves the owner's ability to relicense and
  commercialize, including in enterprise offerings.

## Order of Precedence

In case of conflict:

1. a separately executed written agreement signed by the copyright
   holder;
2. the root `LICENSE` (Apache 2.0, for use of the core) and `CLA.md`
   (for contributions);
3. all other documents in this directory.

## Templates Are Not Grants

Any document or section marked as a template (including Exhibit A of
`CLA.md`) is inactive until completed and validly executed. No
commercial or enterprise grant exists merely because a template is
present in this repository.

## Status

further step is required for them to apply. Governing law for the CLA
is the law of the Russian Federation.
